Our Domains

Arts and Grpahics

Pixel wizards who bring ideas to life, Arts N Graphics creates stunning visuals that demand attention. From bold covers to striking layouts, their designs make every project unforgettable. They don't just design-they mesmerize.

Writing

The wordsmiths of the student body, the Writing Department spins stories, poetry, and humor into pure magic. Their imagination leaps off the page, leaving readers captivated and inspired. They're the soul of every narrative.

Photography & Videography

Masters of moments, the PAV team captures memories that turn into timeless masterpieces. With each click, they transform the ordinary into extraordinary, weaving stories that speak louder than words. Their lens is a portal to visual poetry.

Admin & HR

The backbone of the organization, Admin & HR ensures every idea comes to life with precision. From securing permissions to managing resources and yearbook printing, they make the impossible seem effortless. Strategy meets magic in their expert hands.

Content

The Content Team crafts engaging stories and impactful messages that connect ideas to the audience. From newsletters to social media, they ensure every word aligns with the club's vision and brings creativity to life

Conceptual

The creative think tank of the club, the Conceptual Team decides the themes for newsletters, yearbooks, and reels. From crafting columns to brainstorming fresh ideas, they're the masterminds behind every concept that shapes our vision

Technical

The digital backbone of the team, Technical handles everything from websites to archives with finesse. They streamline data and platforms, ensuring creativity flows without a hitch. Technology is their craft, precision their art.

Social Media

Creates engaging content and helps you live the dream of becoming a youtuber or influencer on a smaller scale. They turn ideas into impactful posts, creating magic that resonates with every audience.
